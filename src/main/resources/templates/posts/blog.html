Wanna Fail Blog?!?
Active Fail is a blogging site where you can anonymously post your Fails in Active pursuit to success. Failing isn't a bad thing, in fact we see it as a great opportunity to share and connect with others. Failure is an opportunity to stay humble, find humor, and come together.
